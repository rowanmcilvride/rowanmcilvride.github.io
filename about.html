ROWAN MCILVRIDE
Work About Contact

Rowan McIlvride is a composer whose work explores perception, memory, fragility, and the physicality of sound. Her practice often draws on field recordings and close collaboration with musicians, dancers, and artists.

Her music has been performed internationally at festivals and venues including Dartington Summer School, Blackheath Halls, and the Queen’s House.

She is currently based in Malaysia and works across composition, collaboration, and curatorial projects.
